I use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data to improve the app. This data helps me understand how users interact with the app and make informed decisions about feature development and user experience improvements.

This data is collected anonymously and cannot be used to identify individual users.

Personal Information and Health Data

I do not collect, use, save, or have access to any of your personal data recorded in the Migrania app for iOS, except for the anonymous usage data mentioned above.

Settings and personal data related to the Migrania app are stored only on your device. You might be asked to provide access to your health data to log symptoms and track your migraines within the app. This health data is stored securely on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers. I don't process or have access to this information.

If you enter any data such as headache symptoms or medication usage, it is saved locally on your device and is not shared with anyone unless you choose to share it.
